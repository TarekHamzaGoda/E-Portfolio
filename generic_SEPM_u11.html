Unit 11

Learning Outcomes: Future Trends

This unit encompasses three key areas: Open Systems, where automated testing, building, and releasing processes are integral; Risk and Knowledge Management, involving a comprehensive understanding of Desired Business, Organisational Solution, and Technical Solution; and Planning for Change, emphasizing the Project Manager's role as a change agent. I Think approaches like Empirical Rational, based on rational adoption, Power-coercive, involving imposed decisions, and Normative Re-Education, emphasizing social influence or collaborative environments, are highlighted as strategies within the context of change management. Overall, this unit provides an insight on the future roles of project management.

Assignment 1 Part 2 Team Evaluation: Cost Calculator Application

The assignment requirements were divided according the unique interests of each team member. In this specific task, responsibilities were organized into GUI, Calculation algorithm, presentation, and system requirements. Each team member efficiently delivered their assigned task with outstanding quality. The scheduled weekly meetings played a crucial role in nurturing discussions on contributed ideas and in assessing the advancements made by each team member in their respective tasks.

Key Requrierments:-

  1. GUI: Tkinters is used to build the application main graphical interface
  2. CLI: CMD library is used to provide terminal navigation
  3. JSON: System components, specs and costs are recorded in editable JSON format
  4. Calculation: COCOMO and (LOC) where utilized as the main form of caculation methods.
